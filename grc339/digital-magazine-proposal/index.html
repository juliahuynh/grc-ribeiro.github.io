Introduction

Taste: Southern California

Taste: Southern California is a monthly American food and entertaining magazine that typically contains entertaining ideas, restaurant recommendations of trendy food spots within Southern California. Food impacts all areas of our life from health and finances to travel and family as well as our social lives. The focus of our magazine is to inspire and encourage discovery by showcasing the most popular spots to dine in at.
