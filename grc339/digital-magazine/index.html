Great Eats at Happy Hour

When it comes to bites and drinks at discounted prices in LA, you might have to skip the clock. Certaintly, don't pass up and compromise on quality, thanks to the wide range of top-notch restaurants with happy hour bargains. Check out spots in bustling neighborhoods such as Culver City and Santa Monica. These great restaurants will never dissapoint!

LA's Best Vegan Food Trucks

There’s a food truck for every taste in this city and many - including Downtown Dogs, India Jones Chow Truck, and Green Truck on the Go - have decent plant-based offerings. Going vegan for lunch one day will be a great opportunity to learn more about nutrition and cooking and the ingredients that have been freshly picked everyday. Ultimately, with all the endless options avaliable at the venue, trying these food trucks will significantly improve your diet.

The secert behind Margarita concoctions

These days, Margaritas can be found in many creative variations using a handful of different tequilas along with fresh fruit, herbs, and spices. You can't go wrong with any of the unique flavors that people have been creating at home and in the restaurant.

Ice Cream Cravings

LA's original ice cream shop has been serving locals and tourists for decades, and its rose-infused ice cream still tastes like springtime. There are a handful or rotating seasonal flavors that have folks swinging through a few nights for a taste.

Gelato Galore

One of the most richest, denser, and smoother desserts relative to Ice cream, there are plenty of spots to visit that maintains the Italian origin. Many interesting and unique flavors.

Beach and Brew

Simple is sometimes, better which is why Beach and Brew's straightforward approach to happy hour is appreciated. Great food aside, the discounted wine and beer are a bonus.

Taco Bucket List

The Taco is the perfect food, often made with a nutritious whole grain corn tortilla base and an incredible range of ingredients and unique textures. You can eat tacos reguarly and never get bored of them.

LA's Hidden Bars

While there are plenty of bars to visit, each space is a worthwhile venue to crack the code - whether it’s to drink, dance and much more. Whether it's karaoke, cigars, a glowing dance floor, or simply a hard-to-find entrance, these bars have surprises waiting for you.

The Ultimate KBBQ

Ready to taste some mouthwatering meats? If you haven't hopped on the Korean BBQ trend by now, this is your guide to Korean BBQ that will range from meats to banchan to drinks that will leave you wanting more.
