Introduction

The Thread Shed

The Thread Shed is a secondhand clothing store. All of the clothes sold at the store are pre-owned, washed, and ready for a new home. The clothes are sourced from donations and thrift shops, and they are all handpicked and tailored for the target consumer.

Target audience

The people using this site are mostly made up of Millenials and Generation Z.

Their primary task is to look for clothes that they want to buy, and their goal is to purchase and wear the clothes.
